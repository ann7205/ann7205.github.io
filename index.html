source: The conversation

My dream of living in a tiny house in the future


pic source: from google images

I’ve never thought one day that I would see tiny house as an option in my life. Not before I realized how easy and comfy it could be after I had been living in a studio apartment. It actually took me a while to adopt this small space, since I’ve never experienced of living in the apartment especially a studio one, I got a bit depressed by the narrow space and wondered why people are able to deal with such environment in a urban city.

Now I’ve been living here for almost 5 years and I can tell what benefits of living in a tiny house:

  1. Easy to clean. I think this reason is self-explained. It saves more time to do house chores for sure.
  2. Minimise consumption habits. Restricted storage and space help you to re-evaluate your life choices, reduce superfluous stuff. Not only encouraging a minimalist lifestyle but also enhancing a quality one.
  3. A perfect living condition with sustainable and reclaimed material. Such as solar power, rainwater etc.
  4. less noise and light from human made pollution.

During this catastrophic bushfire was happening in Australia. I don’t know how many people understand the impact of climate change with alarming consequence. But I do realize the importance of having your own land in this century. Not for business use but a secured land for autarky. As the Podcast-Business Daily that I listened to today, soil erosion and soil degradation will eventually result in lack of food for population. Having land and growing food in a natural way, cutting back on fertilizers and pesticides, giving some time for it to restore. We are now living in a dystopian world, and it’s imaginable that the future will be even tougher for our generation .

Therefore, my plan is to build a tiny house on my own land and living in a self-sufficient lifestyle in the near future.

習慣養成，目標養成計畫

學習是一件持續不斷改善的過程

我一直是學習力普普的人，從小到大的分數都是一般或中上，沒有特別突出。唯一〝突出〞的大概就是數學和理化，因為實在是爛的可以…XD

不知道大家是否和我一樣有類似的情況，閱讀的時候常常會有某些相關的想法也一起一股腦的冒出來，問題是腦子的轉速太快，所以即便事後想要把當下的心情或論點闡述出來也是變得很零碎，沒一開始的流暢。因此，我決定無論如何都要開始練習把自己的讀後感或是學習後的理解像草食動物一樣的反芻，反覆咀嚼後並記錄下來，作為日後的參考和觀察。

近日同時閱讀了幾本相關的書籍，一是精準學習，二是深度工作力，三是本質思考，四是真希望我20歲就懂的事。目前雖還在慢慢閱讀中，不過的確發現每本書探討的中心思想都很類似，所以也更值得探討和改進我的現況。同精準學習所述，如果讀了一本書卻沒有因此改善了自己某些行為，那麼其實自己並沒有學到任何東西，也沒有成長。
